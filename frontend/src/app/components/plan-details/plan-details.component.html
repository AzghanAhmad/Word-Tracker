Writing Strategy

Steady

Write an equal amount every single day. Consistent and predictable progress throughout your project.

Front-load

Start with high intensity and taper off towards the end. Bite the bullet early and finish with momentum.

Back-load

Start small and increase your pace as you approach the deadline. Rising to the challenge and finishing strong.

Mountain

Build up to a peak intensity mid-project, then gradually slow down. Effort concentrated in the middle.

Valley

Fast start, quiet middle, and a strong finish. Effort at the beginning and end with a restful period in between.

Oscillating

Alternate between high-intensity and low-intensity periods. Mixed loads for varied pacing.

Randomly

Every day is a surprise. Pseudo-random distribution that stays consistent unless recalculated.
